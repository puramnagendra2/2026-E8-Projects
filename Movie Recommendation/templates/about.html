About Movie Recommender

Movie Recommender is an advanced content-based filtering system that helps you discover movies you'll love based on your preferences and viewing history.

Our algorithm analyzes movie features like genre, director, cast, and plot to find similar movies that match your taste.

Our Technology

We use content-based filtering, a sophisticated machine learning approach that analyzes movie attributes to find patterns and similarities.

Unlike collaborative filtering which relies on user ratings, our system focuses on the content itself, making it perfect for discovering niche films and overcoming the "cold start" problem for new users.
